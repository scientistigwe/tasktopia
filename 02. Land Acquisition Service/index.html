How to Get Started

1. Register

  • Visit the LandLens website or download the LandLens app from your app store.
  • Click on the "Sign Up" button.
  • Fill out the registration form with your details such as name, email address, and password.
  • Verify your email address if required.

2. Discover Properties

  • Once you're signed in, you can browse through the listings available on LandLens.
  • Use the search filters to narrow down your options based on location, property type, price range, etc.
  • If you're a property owner and want to list your property:
    • Click on the "List Your Property" button.
    • Fill out the listing form with details about your property including description, photos, amenities, and pricing.

3. Complete Checklists

  • Before proceeding further, ensure that you have completed all necessary checklists.
  • For property seekers, this might include checking the property details, location, amenities, and rental terms.
  • For property owners, this might include verifying the accuracy of the listing details and ensuring all necessary information is provided.

4. Customize Services and Payment

  • Choose the service modules you require for your property transaction. This could include services like property management, rental agreements, or property inspections.
  • Select a payment plan that fits your needs and budget. LandLens may offer different pricing tiers based on the level of service and features you require.

5. Relax

  • Once you've completed the necessary steps and selected your service and payment plan, you can sit back and relax.
  • LandLens will handle the process of connecting property seekers with property owners, facilitating transactions, and providing any necessary support along the way.

6. Await Property Handover

  • If you're a property seeker, you can now wait for responses from property owners or agents regarding your inquiries or booking requests.
  • If you're a property owner, you can wait for potential tenants or buyers to show interest in your property and proceed with the rental or sale process.
