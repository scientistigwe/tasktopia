Weather Warriors: Ultimate Tournament

Aim of the Game:

Engage in elemental battles while adapting to dynamic weather conditions. Players select a city, a hero, and enter their name, then compete to reduce their opponent's life points to zero.


Game Setup:
  • Select Continent: Use the dropdown menu to select a continent.
  • Select City: The city dropdown is populated based on the selected continent. Choose a city.
  • Initialize: The game fetches weather data for the selected city.
  • Choose Hero & Enter Name: Select your hero and enter your name to start the game.

Gameplay:
  • Make a Move: Choose one of five moves (rock, paper, scissors, spock, lizard).
  • Determine Outcome: The outcome is determined by the move and current weather conditions.

Weather Effects:

Each weather condition affects moves differently, providing advantages or disadvantages.

  • Weather Types:
    • Thunderstorm, Drizzle, Rain, Snow, Atmosphere (fog), Clear, Clouds.
  • Impact:
    • Health changes based on move and weather compatibility.

Move Mechanics:
  • Rock: Wins against scissors and lizard; loses to paper and spock.
  • Paper: Wins against rock and spock; loses to scissors and lizard.
  • Scissors: Wins against paper and lizard; loses to rock and spock.
  • Spock: Wins against rock and scissors; loses to paper and lizard.
  • Lizard: Wins against spock and paper; loses to rock and scissors.

Health Points:

Both players start with 100 life points. Correct move choices reduce opponent's life points based on weather impact. Each move has a health change value, influenced by the current weather.


Winning the Round:

A player wins the round by reducing the opponentâ€™s life points to zero. If both players have equal life points at the end, the round is a draw.


Game Continuation:

The game can be played in multiple rounds. Players can view their life points and move outcomes after each round.


Declaring the Winner:

After a set number of rounds, the player with the highest total life points wins. A summary of moves and weather effects is displayed at the end.


Additional Features:
  • Hints/Clues: Use game-provided hints to make better moves.
  • Historical Data: View past weather data to predict move impacts.
  • Visual Elements: Icons and images represent different weather conditions.
  • Multiplayer Mode: Play against other players to see who makes the best use of moves and weather conditions.

Rules:
  • Fair Play: Make moves based on available information.
  • Respect: Maintain sportsmanship in multiplayer mode.
  • No Cheating: Manipulation of game data is prohibited.

Enjoy playing Weather Warriors: Ultimate Tournament where strategy, adaptability, and knowledge of weather are key to becoming the ultimate warrior!
